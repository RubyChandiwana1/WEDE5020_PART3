About Our Organization

Majita Care NPO is dedicated to raising awareness about men’s health issues, focusing on hypertension prevention, wellness education, and safe medical practices. We provide free screenings, educational workshops, and support networks to empower men and their families.

Our History

Founded in 2015, Majita Care NPO began as a small initiative to educate men about preventive health measures. Today, we are a trusted organization serving thousands across Johannesburg and surrounding communities, with outreach programs that continue to grow every year.

Our Mission

To empower men and communities with knowledge and resources that improve overall health outcomes.

Our Vision

A world where every man has access to health education and preventive care.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity: Honest, transparent, and accountable in all actions.
  • Compassion: Caring deeply about the health and wellbeing of every individual.
  • Collaboration: Working with communities, partners, and volunteers to achieve meaningful outcomes.
  • Excellence: Striving for the highest quality in education, services, and support.

Our Programs & Services

We offer a range of free or low-cost programs to promote men’s health:

  • Blood pressure and diabetes screenings
  • HIV prevention and counseling services
  • Mental health support groups
  • Nutrition and fitness workshops
  • Mobile health clinics for rural outreach

Community Impact

Since our founding, we have reached over 5,000 beneficiaries through health workshops, counseling sessions, and outreach programs. Our initiatives include HIV prevention, male circumcision awareness, and hypertension screening campaigns. In 2024 alone, we conducted 120 free health clinics across Gauteng Province.

Our Partners

We collaborate with local clinics, government health departments, educational institutions, and other NGOs to expand the reach and impact of our programs.
