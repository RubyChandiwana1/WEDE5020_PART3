Our Health Services

At Majita Care NPO, we are dedicated to providing health services that are accessible, culturally respectful, and medically safe. We focus on men’s health, particularly in areas where awareness, access, and trust in healthcare need strengthening.

Medical Male Circumcision

Majita Care NPO offers safe, professional, and accessible medical male circumcision services. This important preventative healthcare measure reduces the risk of infections and improves hygiene.

Why Circumcision?

Circumcision can help reduce the risk of HIV and other sexually transmitted infections. It also promotes better hygiene and prevents certain medical conditions.

Our Approach

  • Safety: Adhering to the highest medical standards.
  • Comfort: Painless procedures using local anesthesia.
  • Education: Detailed information before and after the procedure.
  • Confidentiality: All services are private and discreet.

Eligibility

Services are available for males of all ages — infants, adolescents, and adults. Whether for cultural or medical reasons, we offer support at every stage.

HIV & AIDS Support

Majita Care NPO offers holistic HIV & AIDS services focusing on prevention, education, testing, treatment, and ongoing support.

Our Services Include:

  • Testing and Counseling: Confidential HIV screening and professional advice.
  • Treatment: Access to ART and medical support.
  • Prevention: PrEP, condoms, educational materials, and safer sex workshops.
  • Support: Emotional counseling, peer groups, and family guidance.

Why Choose Us?

  • Non-judgmental Care: Safe space for all individuals.
  • Complete Confidentiality: We protect your privacy.
  • Community-Centered: We engage local leaders and groups in our outreach.
